Key Features

Personalized Speaking Assessment

Evaluating an individual's speaking ability based on their unique skills and characteristics. Complements other forms of evaluation, like group discussions, oral presentations, and interactive language activities, to provide a holistic view of a person's language proficiency and communication skills.

Real-time feedback and Analysis

Get real-time feedback and corrections on your pronunciation, intonation, and fluency to enhance your speaking performance which enabling you to focus on specific areas for improvement.

Customized Practice Exercises

Tailored learning activities designed to meet the specific needs and preferences of individual learners. Particularly valuable in self-paced learning environments, online education platforms, and adaptive learning systems, where individualization is essential for effective learning outcomes.
